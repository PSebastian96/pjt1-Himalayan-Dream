About us

We are a buddhist community based in the city of Bristol.

Join our meditation and yoga sessions, meet new people and discover the path to your spiritual journey by joining us.

By joining our community, you will become one of our community garden member, where we teach our members urban gardening and grow our own food that you can take home or donate to local charities.
